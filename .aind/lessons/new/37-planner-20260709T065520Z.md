## Observation
Both categorization-domain.md and testing.md mandate that the categorization service be unit-tested with "real sample e-mails captured as fixtures," and story #37's ACs lean entirely on "the sample emails provided during implementation" plus a 14-row reference-examples table — yet the repo contains no sample-e-mail corpus, no documented source for one, and no convention for where such fixtures live or how they are anonymised. The only in-repo source of real raw Graph messages is the throwaway MailDebug dump from story #36. So a planner (and coder) has to invent both the fixture-capture path and the storage/anonymisation convention from scratch, and this same ambiguity will recur for every downstream categorization story (#38/#39 and any rule refinement). The cause is that the rules specify a fixture-driven test practice without the project ever establishing the fixture corpus or a location/format convention to back it.

## Observation
frontend-architecture.md's placement note states src/services/ is "reserved for the categorization business logic," but the codebase already keeps I/O service modules there (src/services/graph, src/services/mail) and this story added src/services/projectMap. The rule text and the established code convention disagree: services/ is in practice the home for the service layer generally (graph/mail/categorization/projectMap), not categorization alone. The phrasing reads as if a new non-categorization service does not belong under services/, which is misleading — the intent of that note is only to keep pure colocated helpers out of services/, not to forbid other services.

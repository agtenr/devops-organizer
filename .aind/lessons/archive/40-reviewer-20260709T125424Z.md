## Observation
Two consecutive cold-review passes flagged the same human-directed addition (a Playwright harness + E2E specs) as blocking "scope creep." The addition was made at the human's explicit on-PR instruction after they found live defects the plan's test strategy failed to anticipate. Because the cold reviewer re-grounds from the plan + rules + diff, and the human's steering lives only in PR comments (not in the merged plan it treats as the spec), the reviewer structurally reads any human-directed deviation-from-plan as unsanctioned scope creep and gates on it — until it reads the human's own PR comment and reverses. This recurs whenever a human directs a mid-PR change that deviates from the merged plan: the coldness that makes the gate valuable also makes it re-litigate human-approved deviations that aren't reflected in the artifacts it grounds from.
